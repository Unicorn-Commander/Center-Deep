About Center Deep

Our Mission

Center Deep is committed to providing a private, comprehensive search experience that respects user privacy while delivering high-quality results from multiple sources.

How It Works

As a metasearch engine, Center Deep queries multiple search engines simultaneously and aggregates their results. This approach provides:

  • More comprehensive search results
  • Reduced bias from any single search engine
  • Enhanced privacy through proxy searches
  • No user tracking or profiling

Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously:

  • No cookies or tracking
  • No search history stored
  • No user accounts required
  • All searches are anonymous

Technology

Center Deep is built with modern web technologies and is based on open-source principles. We believe in transparency and user freedom.
